Day 1: June 20th

Hutchison Square, Third Thursday

Join us to Meet and Greet everyone! This is being held in conjunction with Third Thursday sponsored by DREAM Community Partners. If you haven’t visited Summerville recently, you’re in for a treat since the city’s historic downtown is transformed into a unique local market with regional artists, crafters, and entertainers. During this period, the unique stores, boutiques, and eateries will be open for all to enjoy. There will be a table on Hutchison Square with the balloons 84! Dress is casual! There is plenty of FREE parking throughout the downtown including the town garage located behind Town Hall. Traffic is re-routed from 4:00 until 9:00 from the following streets: Little Main Street, W Richardson to Central. 

Located in W. Richardson & Short Central
Rain or Shine

Day 2: June 21st

Candlelite Pavilion at Summerville Country Club

Let’s enjoy great food, an open bar and dancing to ‘80’s music while we catch up with our classmates at one of Summerville’s nostalgic places! A few of our favorite teachers will join us as we remember so many good times. Dress is casual.

We will also have a tribute for our classmates who have passed.
Parking is available on site.

Located at 400 Country Club Blvd, Summerville, SC 29483

Day 3: June 22nd

Memorial Stadium & McKissick Museum

This day will include a walking tour of the McKissick Museum and the Stadium. The stadium has been renovated and includes a statue of Coach and Mrs. McKissick. The museum is a must see! It opened in the fall of 2023, and we are in for a treat ~ there is lots of memorabilia, including all ten state championship trophies won under McKissick’s tutelage. We can even click on a button to re-live our senior year football season! The Dorchester Two Educational Foundation provides opportunities for alumni and the community to support the McKissick Museum by purchasing custom engraved bricks for the walkway.
Parking is available on site.

Located at 805 S. Main Street, Summerville, SC 29483
