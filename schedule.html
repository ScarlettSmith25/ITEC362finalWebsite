40 years of the class of 84! This is an exciting time for us all, and what better way to celebrate than with a reunion at the place where it all started. The Summerville High School Class of 84 Reunion will take place from June 20th to June 22nd. Go check out all of the fun events that we have planned, and buy your tickets! As always, GO GREEN WAVE!

Day 1: June 20th

This is will be the arrival day for most of the attending alumni, therefore the event will be very casual. The event is the Third Thursday in Hutchinson Square for free live music in downtown Summerville. The event will start at 5:30 pm.

Day 2: June 21st

This event will be at the Candlelight Pavilion at the Miler Country Club.

Day 3: June 22nd

There will be a walking tour of the McKissick Museum and Stadium.
